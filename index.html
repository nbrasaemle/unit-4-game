Reunite the Fellowship!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are characters from "The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring" below. By clicking on a set of characters you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each set of characters is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each set of characters.

If NaN appears reload the page and play again.
